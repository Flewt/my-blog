What didn't go well?

Each time I needed to switch focus to something new and plan each step involved with that, eg moving from solution to brand design, I would get overwhelmed and not want to do it. I still have a lot of coding to catch up on. I think I can help my difficulties with the transition between phases of the design process by having templates and checklists on hand ahead of time. I think developing a more regular check-in schedule with the lectures will help my with organisation.
